Choose a Retailer or Service

Pick a store or service to which you want to send prepaid credit. We work with well know companies in Mexico and you will find a retailer near them. All you need is their zip code.

Send Funds

Select an ammount and recepient. Your family gets notified about the transfer and receives a PIN code or a confirmation telling them a service has been paid.

Shop

If you paid for a service, the credit will be added to the account immediatly. If you sent money to a store your family shops as they normally would and use the pin code to pay for their items.
